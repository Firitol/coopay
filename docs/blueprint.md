# **App Name**: Coopay Market

## Core Features:

- User Authentication & Authorization: Secure login/signup for Buyers, Sellers, and Admin/Bank Staff, including OTP and role-based access control.
- Product Management: Sellers can list, edit, and manage their products (name, price, description, images, category), while Buyers can browse, search, and filter these listings, with product data securely stored in the database.
- Shopping Cart & Order Placement: Buyers can add products to a shopping cart, review their selection, and proceed to order placement.
- Coopay Payment Integration: Simulated or integrated Coopay payment gateway for processing payments from bank accounts or mobile wallets, including transaction history and status tracking.
- Seller Dashboard: A dedicated dashboard for sellers to add/edit products and view incoming orders and their status.
- Admin & Bank Staff Panel: Tools for administrators and bank staff to manage users (activate/deactivate), monitor key transactions, and view basic reports.
- Essential Notifications: Automated email/SMS notifications for crucial updates like order confirmations and payment status changes.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A sophisticated and trustworthy deep forest green (#266B3F), symbolizing growth and reliability for the banking and market context.
- Background color: A subtle, desaturated light green-gray (#F2F6F3), ensuring a clean and professional appearance suitable for transaction-heavy pages.
- Accent color: A vibrant spring green (#84CA4F), chosen to provide clear contrast and draw attention to interactive elements and calls to action without being overly aggressive.
- Headline and body font: 'Inter' (sans-serif), selected for its modern, highly legible, and neutral aesthetic, ensuring clarity across various screen sizes for a diverse user base.
- Utilize a consistent set of crisp, minimalist line icons that align with a modern and clean design language for intuitive navigation.
- Employ a responsive grid-based layout for product listings and dashboards, optimizing content display for seamless usability on both desktop and mobile devices.
- Incorporate subtle, functional animations for feedback on user interactions, such as adding items to cart or submitting forms, enhancing the perceived responsiveness of the application.
